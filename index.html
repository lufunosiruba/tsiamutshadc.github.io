Welcome to Tsia-Mutsha Drop-in-Centre

Tsia-Mutsha drop in center provides a safe haven for children, offering them love, comfort, and tools they need to succeed. Through various programs and activities, children receive emotional support, educational assistance, and opportunities for personal growth. Our caring staff and voluteers ensure that each child feels a sense of belonging and encouragement, helping them to build confidence and resilience for a brighter future.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment where individuals and families in need can find the help and resources they need to thrive.

Our History

Founded in 2011, the Drop-In Center has grown from a small community initiative into a comprehensive support system for those in need. Over the years, we have expanded our services to meet the changing needs of our community, offering everything from basic necessities to counseling and educational programs.

Our Services

At our Drop-In Center, we offer a range of services to support individuals in need. Below are the key services we provide:

Counseling Services

We offer free and confidential counseling services for individuals and families.

Our professional counselors are available to assist with mental health support, crisis intervention, and guidance through challenging times. Appointments are not required; walk-ins are welcome.

Basic Needs Assistance

We provide essential items such as food, clothing, and hygiene products.

Our center offers food pantries, clothing drives, and hygiene kits to those in need. These resources are available daily on a first-come, first-served basis.

Workshops and Educational Programs

Join our workshops to learn new skills and improve your well-being.

We offer various workshops, including job training, financial literacy, and wellness programs. Check our Events page for upcoming workshops and how to register.

Community Support Groups

Connect with others through our support groups.

We facilitate support groups for different needs, including addiction recovery, grief counseling, and peer support. These groups are a safe space for sharing and support.
